Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to license my test servers?

No, only production servers count. You can have as many testing and staging servers are you want.

Can I distribute UniDoc with my application to my customers?

Yes, all licenses are Royalty-Free.

Can I resassign unused Business Developer License?

Yes, Business Developer licenses are floating and can be re-assigned when developers leave a project.

Do I need to renew my subscription after the one year is up?

Our licenses are perpetual, so you don't need to. If you don't, you are not unable to use update to releases released after the subscription is expired. To keep receiving updates and commercial support, you must renew the subscription.

Can I evaluate the product before buying a license?

Yes, feel free the use the open source license while evaluating and then buy a license from us when you are ready.
